*Delivery: unhurried lines over fast visuals — the contrast reads premium. Record at −16 LUFS, light compression, no reverb.*

## 3. Animation & transition instructions

- **Cuts, not dissolves** between scenes 2–8 (2-frame 8% luma pop on each cut for percussive feel)
- Entrances: 280ms cubic ease-out; slides 40px; scale settles from 1.05
- Counters: 96-score in 1.4s ease-out; bars with 8% overshoot then settle
- Camera: slow 2% push-in within each scene (adds life without motion sickness)
- Terminal fade: 0.8s to black; never crossfade UI over UI

## 4. Music style
Minimal dark-electronic pulse, 100 BPM: sub-bass root (A), soft four-on-floor kick from 8.5s (the "turn"), airy pad above. Swell +15% into CTA, kick stops at 26s — pad resolves alone. Reference: enterprise keynote openers; never louder than −20 dB under VO.
