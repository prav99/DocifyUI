# Docify Landing Films — 30-Second Marketing Series
### Scene-by-scene plan, voiceover scripts, on-screen text, animation & sound direction

Three connected ~30s films, one per use case, zero overlap. Each: hook (0–5s) → problem/setup → fast product demo with animated callouts → benefit close → pointer to the next film. Rendered as self-playing narrated players in the real product UI style; voiceover via the site's narration engine; captions always visible; ambient SaaS score with soft interaction SFX (clicks, confirms, completion chime). Music ducks under narration.

---

## Music & sound design (all films)
Light professional SaaS bed: warm major-key pads + soft pluck arpeggio, ~112 BPM feel, −20 dB under narration; interaction SFX at the moments marked above; completion chime at each film's resolution; music resolves cleanly at the close. No loud drops, no distortion.

## Series connection
01 → points to 03 (AI Readiness) → points to 02 (Generation) → points back to 01 (Automation). Each pointer is a clickable card that scrolls the visitor to the recommended film.
